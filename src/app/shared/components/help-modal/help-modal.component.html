Mastering Attendance Tracking
1. Location Verification

The system requires GPS verification. When you click "Clock In", your browser will ask for location permission. **You must "Allow" this** to proceed. Ensure you are within the designated university zone.

2. Digital Signatures

A clear digital signature is required for every entry and exit. Use your mouse or touch screen. If you make a mistake, use the "Clear" button and redraw before saving.

3. Forget to Clock Out?

If you leave without clocking out, your session remains "Active". The next time you log in, you must complete the previous session's clock-out before starting a new day. Continuous "Active" sessions may require supervisor intervention.

Leave Management Workflow

Pro-Tip: Always submit leave requests at least 48 hours in advance for planned absences.

1
Filling the Form

Specify the dates and the reason for absence clearly. This helps your supervisor make a quick decision.

3

Troubleshooting Hub
System says "Location Access Denied"?

Click the lock icon in your browser's address bar, reset permissions for this site, and refresh the page. Ensure your device's global GPS is ON.

Why can't I see my intern's reports?

Ensure the intern is correctly assigned to your Department in their profile settings. Admins can verify these links in the User Management portal.

How do I generate a Monthly Report?

Navigate to the "Reports" module, select the desired month, and click "Export PDF". This will include all verified signatures for the period.

Session timeout issues?

For security, sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity. Simply log back in to refresh your token.
